Shadatul Yasmin Binti Mohamed Shahren is currently a student of Diploma in Information Management in Semester 7 at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), pursuing her studies as a part-time student while working full-time as a Head of Document Control at Innovative Synergy Solutions Sdn Bhd, Kuala Lumpur.

With 8 years of experience as a Document Controller, she is committed to maintaining high standards of document control quality in accordance with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 15489 practices.
