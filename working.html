| Working Experience

Shadatul Yasmin currently serves as a Head of Document Control at Innovative Synergy Solutions Sdn Bhd (ISS), a company specializing in EPCC process packages. She is responsible for managing project documentation, coordinating document submissions, ensuring compliance with client requirements, and maintaining document control procedures throughout project lifecycles.

With over 8 years of experience in document control, she has worked extensively in the oil and gas industry, handling large volumes of engineering and project documentation for local and international clients.

Her expertise includes Electronic Document Management Systems (EDMS), document tracking, records management, transmittal preparation, quality documentation, and ensuring compliance with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 15489 standards.

Throughout her career, she has successfully supported multidisciplinary project teams by ensuring accurate document control processes, timely submissions, and effective communication between clients, vendors, and internal departments.
